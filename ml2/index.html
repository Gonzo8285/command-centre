The bridge protocol — visible, not magic

Every cross-seat message is a file with YAML frontmatter and an ACK window tied to priority. P0 ACK in 10 minutes or it escalates. P3 needs no ACK. Heartbeats every 30 minutes. Memory mirrors one-way, seat-owns-own. The whole protocol is one document — readable in 5 minutes — and is the difference between agents-as-chatbots and agents-as-operations-layer.
